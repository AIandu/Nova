## Community Intelligence Hub

## GuardianEye
AI-powered security platform that runs on existing security cameras — detects weapons, intrusions, fights, falls, or wandering in real time and delivers verified alerts to staff or first responders. Positioned for schools, elder care, and small business security. Category: Public Safety / Defense-adjacent.
